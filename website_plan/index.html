Website Plan: ACI Tech Hub

Site Name

ACI Tech Hub

The name "ACI Tech Hub" combines the founder's initials with the term "Tech Hub" to emphasize a central place for technology learning and support. It clearly signals a focus on digital empowerment tailored for beginners and small business owners in Nigeria.

Site Purpose

The website provides information about ACI Tech Hub’s mission to teach digital skills and offer tech support. It introduces the services offered such as digital literacy training, coding workshops, website setup consultations, and business technology support. The site also facilitates community engagement through event registration and contact forms.

Scenarios

  • How can I register for the next digital literacy training session?
  • Where can I find ACI Tech Hub’s location and contact information for business tech support?

Color Schema

  • Tech Blue (#013355) - Used for headings, navigation, buttons, and links to convey trust and modernity.
  • Light Gray (#f4f6f8) - Used as the background color for the entire site for a clean and soft look.

Typography

  • Poppins - Used for all headings (h1, h2) to provide a modern and friendly appearance.
  • Roboto - Used for body text, paragraphs, lists, and forms for readability on all devices.
